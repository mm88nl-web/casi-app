# Brand Context

## Brand
- **Name**: Casi (casi.gg)
- **Category**: Creator economy / live streaming tools
- **Description**: A platform where viewers can pay to put live overlays ("beams") on a streamer's broadcast — approval-gated, real-time, visual interaction between viewers and streamers
- **Stage**: Early (live product, growing streamer base)
- **Website**: https://casi.gg

## Audience
- **Primary Audience**: Live streamers on Twitch and Kick (and YouTube) who want a new interactive revenue stream from viewers
- **Secondary Audience**: Viewers who want to stand out and interact with streamers in real time
- **Key Problem**: Streamers have limited interactive monetization options beyond donations/subscriptions; viewers have no fun, visual way to get on stream
- **Their Language**: "on stream", "beam", "overlay", "live", "viewers", "go live", "drops", "bits", "subs", "approval", "chat"

## Positioning
- **Differentiation**: Streamer approval-gated (nothing appears without the streamer allowing it), dual payment rails (Solana crypto + Stripe card), visual overlay format rather than just a notification — it's actually *on screen*, not in a notification panel
- **Competitors**: Streamlabs (donations/alerts), StreamElements (tips/alerts), Ko-fi, Buy Me a Coffee — all text/audio notifications, none are approval-gated visual overlays
- **Market Position**: Niche — creator-economy SaaS targeting interactive live streaming monetization

## Brand Personality
- **Personality Words**: Direct, minimal, playful, creator-first, modern
- **Tone**: Casual and punchy — the landing page tagline is literally "Get on a live stream." Short sentences, no fluff
- **Visual Identity**: Dark paper (#0C0D11) + teal ink (#0DCFB0), custom logotype with a lozenge mark, serif + grotesque type pairing
- **Voice Admires**: Stripe (clarity + craft), Linear (minimal + confident), Are.na (calm, no hype)

## Values & Mission
- **Core Values**: Creator control, simplicity, real interaction, openness (free tier available)
- **Mission**: Give streamers a new way to monetize live engagement — and give viewers a way to actually get on stream

## Goals
- **Primary Goal**: Grow active streamers using casi.gg for live streams (next 12 months: reach consistent weekly active streamers)
- **Key Metrics**: Streamer signups, streams run via casi.gg, beams sent, conversion from free to paid beams
