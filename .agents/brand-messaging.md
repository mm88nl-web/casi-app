# Casi — Messaging Framework

---

## 01 — CORE MESSAGE

Casi helps live streamers earn from their audience by letting viewers pay to appear on screen — with the streamer approving every one.

---

## 02 — VALUE PROPOSITION

Casi turns your stream into a two-way show. Viewers pay to put their clips, images, or banners live on your broadcast — by the minute or per flash. You approve every beam before it hits the screen. You keep 100%. It runs as an OBS browser source: no downloads, no plugins, no platform lock-in.

---

## 03 — TAGLINE OPTIONS

**Option 1: *"Viewers pay to get on your stream."***
- Style: Functional / direct
- Rationale: States exactly what the product does in 8 words. Lets the idea be the hook. Closest to the current landing page voice.

**Option 2: *"Your stream is real estate. Lease it."***
- Style: Witty / metaphor
- Rationale: Already live in the OG metadata — earns a second look because the metaphor is accurate, not just clever. Works as a secondary headline or email subject line.
- ★ **Recommended for secondary use** — too conceptual to be the first thing someone reads, but memorable enough to be the thing they quote to someone else.

**Option 3: *"Get on a live stream."***
- Style: Aspirational / viewer-facing
- Rationale: Already the homepage tagline. Speaks to the viewer desire. Best for top-of-funnel or viewer acquisition.
- ★ **Recommended as the viewer-facing hook** — keep this on the homepage.

**Option 4: *"Approval-gated. Every beam."***
- Style: Functional / trust signal
- Rationale: Leads with the differentiator most streamers care about: control. Works for mid-funnel copy, feature lists, and ads targeting streamers who've been burned by unmoderated donations.

**Option 5: *"The stream stays yours."***
- Style: Emotional / control
- Rationale: Addresses the underlying anxiety — that adding viewer interaction means losing control of your stream. Reassurance as positioning.

**★ Primary recommended tagline:** *"Viewers pay to get on your stream."* — functional, unambiguous, and lets the listener fill in the "that sounds interesting" for themselves.

## 04 — MESSAGING HIERARCHY

**Level 1 — Brand Headline**
*Viewers pay to get on your stream.*

**Level 2 — Supporting Statement**
*You approve every beam before it hits the screen. You keep 100%. Works with OBS — no downloads.*

---

**Level 3 — Key Messages**

**You're always in control.**
Every beam goes through your approval queue. Nothing appears on your stream without your say-so — not a setting, a hard product guarantee. If you don't approve it, it doesn't run.
*Proof: Approval-gate is built into the escrow flow. Unapproved beams are never charged.*

**Viewers actually appear on your stream.**
This isn't a donation notification. Viewers place clips, images, or banners live on your broadcast — visible to everyone watching, for as long as they paid for. It's on-screen, not in a panel.
*Proof: OBS browser source at 1920×1080, z-ordered above or below camera as you choose.*

**Open escrow. No platform cut.**
The money is held in an Apache 2.0 smart contract on Solana — auditable by anyone — or charged via Stripe. Casi takes no rake on beam revenue. What viewers pay, you keep.
*Proof: Apache 2.0 smart contract on Solana, open source. 100% to the streamer.*

**Free tier. Zero friction to start.**
Free beams exist. You can run casi on your stream before a single viewer pays anything. Paid beams unlock when you're ready — no subscription, no lock-in.
*Proof: Free tier live on casi.gg. No credit card required for the streamer to set up.*

## 05 — AUDIENCE-SPECIFIC MESSAGING

**For streamers (primary):**
- What they care most about: Control over what appears on their stream; new revenue that doesn't feel like begging
- Lead with: Control and the approval gate — *"You decide what makes the cut"*
- Proof points that land hardest: 100% kept, approval-gate is a hard guarantee, OBS browser source (no setup friction)
- Avoid: Crypto-first framing, "passive income" language, anything that sounds like another donation tool

**For viewers (secondary):**
- What they care most about: Actually being seen, interacting beyond chat, getting a reaction from the streamer
- Lead with: *"Get on a live stream"* — the aspiration, not the mechanics
- Proof points that land hardest: It shows on screen for everyone watching, the streamer sees it
- Avoid: Technical details about escrow, pricing mechanics upfront

**For potential partners / press:**
- What they care most about: What's genuinely new here, is the business model sustainable
- Lead with: The category — *"The only approval-gated viewer-to-screen overlay tool"*
- Proof points that land hardest: Open-source escrow, dual payment rails, no platform rake
- Avoid: "We're like X but better" framing

## 07 — THINGS NOT TO SAY

- *"Passive income"* — casi requires the streamer to approve beams. It's active by design. Calling it passive is false and attracts the wrong streamers.
- *"Tip/donate/support"* — these frame casi as a donation tool. It's an interaction tool. Different category.
- *"Viewers can support your stream"* — "support" is Ko-fi / Buy Me a Coffee language. It undersells the product.
- *"Join our community"* — casi has no community product. Don't promise what isn't there.
- *"Powered by blockchain"* — the escrow uses Solana because it's the right technical tool for the job. "Blockchain" as a selling point repels more people than it attracts.
- Any claim about being the "best" or "top" tool — casi can claim to be the *only* approval-gated visual overlay tool. Own that. "Best" is a weak word; "only" is a strong one.
