Casi is in dev preview

The product works end-to-end, but we're holding off on real money until our smart contract audit clears. Here's what that means.

  • ◎
    Solana Devnet · not mainnet
    USDC payments use testnet tokens that have no real value. You can grab free devnet USDC from the faucet to try the flow.
  • €
    Stripe is in test mode
    Card payments route through Stripe's test environment. Cards aren't actually charged — no money moves.
  • ✓
    Audit in progress
    The escrow contract is open-source and under review by an independent auditor. Once the audit clears, we deploy to mainnet and the app handles real funds.
  • ♦
    Casi never holds your money
    Card payments go streamer → Stripe → streamer's Stripe account. USDC sits in an on-chain escrow contract. We're software, not a bank.
